Kochab Cheat Sheet
An honest, human-in-the-loop job search that runs inside Claude. It drafts; you always send. No auto-apply, no scraping behind logins, no fake confidence scores, nothing invented. Say things your own way; the phrases below are examples.
★
KOCHAB
the star you steer by

1 First run (setup)

Point Kochab at a folder and share your resume. It then:
  • interviews you and writes Master_Profile.md, including your honesty guardrails
  • seeds a story bank from your resume
  • researches a target-employer list for your field
  • asks how hands-on you want it (the coaching dial)
Then the scan can run on a schedule. Your data stays in your folder.

2 Find & apply

Scan. "run my job scan"
New roles, deduped against your tracker, each with a fit score, a named gap, a why-you-fit line, and a legitimacy check. Thin days reported honestly.
Draft. "write a cover note for X"
A short note from a real hook and your stories, never a restatement of the posting. You edit and send.
Plan. "what should I study?"
A study plan shaped by your real weekly hours, aimed at one closable gap, with real resources.
Resume. "tailor my resume for X"
A variant built by subtraction from your profile. Never adds anything untrue.

3 Interview & stages

Interview. "prep me for the X interview"
Likely questions mapped to your real stories, questions to ask, thin spots handled honestly. Tuned to the format: screen, panel, or onsite.
Stage guidance. "what does this stage expect?"
What each phase is for and what comes next, plus how to handle references (who to ask, when, how to brief them).

4 Run the whole search

Pipeline. "I applied to X"   "where do I stand?"
One tracker holding every application's stage, next action, and timing, with a status view of what is due and what collides this week.
Momentum. "should I follow up?"   "not interested in X"
Follow-up timing (capped, never pestering), nudges to get drafts sent, post-call capture, one-step decline.

5 Offers & process health

Offer. "I got an offer"   "help me negotiate"
The whole offer weighed against your priorities and floor, and an honestly-anchored counter drafted for you to send. Never fabricated leverage.
Process health. "I got rejected"   "how is my search going?"
Honest rejection recovery, a gracious withdrawal note, and a light read of your own funnel with no false precision.

6 Network (bounded)

Network. "do I know anyone at X?"
Contacts from your own LinkedIn export or a paste, matched only to an employer you intend to apply to, with a warm outreach note to send yourself. Never scraped, never a blanket scan.

7 Coaching dial

"be more hands-on"   "just hold things, let me ask"
Turns how much Kochab nudges and coaches up or down. Light by default. It changes how much Kochab speaks, never how honest it is.

8 How fit is scored

Six sub-scores, 0 to 3 each, shown together with a named gap. Never a bare number or a percentage.
core-skillleveldomain
locationmissionscreens
Strong14–18, no zero on core-skill, level, or screens
Stretch9–13 (or higher with one capping zero)
Skip8 or below, or breaks a hard no
A zero on core-skill, level, or screens caps a role at Stretch, however high the total.

9 The rules that never bend

Never: auto-submits, fills a form, scrapes behind a login, invents a skill or credential, or shows a fit score as an objective number.
Always: tells you the truth. It will call a role a Skip, tell you when you do not clear a screen, and report a day with nothing new. That honesty is the point.
Your data stays in your folder, on your machine, and is never shared.
Kochab drafts, researches, and organizes. You decide and you send, every time.
