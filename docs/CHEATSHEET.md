# Kochab Cheat Sheet

One page. Everything Kochab does, and what to say to trigger it. Kochab drafts; you always send. It never auto-applies, never scrapes behind a login, and never invents anything. Say things in your own words; the phrases below are examples, not commands.

**First run:** point Kochab at a folder and share your resume. It interviews you, writes your `Master_Profile.md` (including your honesty guardrails and how hands-on you want it), seeds a story bank, and researches a target-employer list. After that, the scan can run on a schedule.

| Mode | Say something like | What you get |
|---|---|---|
| **Setup** | "set up my job scan," or share your resume | Guided intake: profile, honesty guardrails, story bank, target employers, intervention level |
| **Scan** | "run my job scan," "what should I apply to" | New roles found and deduped, each with a transparent fit score, a named gap, a why-you-fit line, and a legitimacy check. Thin days reported honestly |
| **Draft** | "write a cover note for [role]," "draft an application email" | A short note built from a real hook and your stories, never a restatement of the posting. You edit and send |
| **Plan** | "what should I study," "how do I qualify for [role]" | A study plan shaped by your real weekly hours, pointed at one closable gap, with real resources and an artifact endpoint |
| **Interview** | "prep me for the [company] interview" | Likely questions mapped to your real stories, questions to ask, thin spots handled honestly, tuned to the format (screen, panel, onsite) |
| **Pipeline** | "I applied to [role]," "where does my search stand," "what's my next step" | One tracker holding every application's stage, next action, and timing, with a status view of what is due and what collides |
| **Momentum** | "should I follow up on [role]," "help me get my drafts out," "I'm not interested in [role]" | Follow-up timing (capped, never pestering), drafted-not-sent nudges, post-call capture, one-step decline |
| **Stage guidance** | "what does this stage expect," "who should I ask for a reference" | What each stage is for and what comes next, plus how to handle references well |
| **Offer** | "I got an offer," "help me negotiate" | The whole offer weighed against your priorities and floor, and an honestly-anchored counter drafted for you to send |
| **Process health** | "I got rejected," "how do I withdraw from [role]," "how is my search going" | Honest rejection recovery, a gracious withdrawal note, and a light read of your own funnel (no false precision) |
| **Network** | "do I know anyone at [employer]," "import my LinkedIn connections" | Contacts from your own export or paste, matched to an employer you intend to apply to, with a warm outreach note to send yourself |
| **Coaching dial** | "be more hands-on," "just hold things and let me ask" | Turns how much Kochab nudges and coaches up or down. Light by default. Never changes how honest it is |

**Where your data lives:** in your folder, on your machine. Profiles, trackers, contacts, and history never leave it and are never shared.

**The one rule that never bends:** Kochab tells you the truth. It will call a role a Skip, tell you that you do not clear a screen, and report a day with nothing new. That honesty is the point.
